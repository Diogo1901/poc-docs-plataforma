# Webhooks

Webhook notifications are essential for ensuring real-time updates and seamless integration with our platform. These notifications are triggered by various events, such as the insertion or update of a property listing or the creation of a new lead. When such events occur, notifications are sent as API calls, providing timely information about the changes.

The structure of these webhook notifications is meticulously designed to facilitate the identification of the event and its association with CasaYes. This ensures that our system and any integrated third-party systems can efficiently process and respond to these updates.

By adhering to a specific format, webhook notifications ensure consistency and reliability. This structured approach allows developers to easily parse the notifications and implement appropriate handling mechanisms. Consequently, webhook notifications play a crucial role in maintaining up-to-date information and enhancing the overall efficiency of property management and lead handling processes on the CasaYes platform.
